Connecting you with your world.

Chattr means that you're always connected to your love ones, wherever they are in the world. And because it uses Facebook, your contacts are already synced.

Core technologies.

Redis is awesome. It's speed and ease of use powers the portion of our site that handles the chat room state storage.

Node.js is the invisible guardian of chattr. It serves the content, sets up the initial connections between you and your friends and runs the services behind the scenes.

WebRTC is a new technology that is used to send the video chat data between you and your friends. It's all encrypted, so that your private conversations remain just that - private.
